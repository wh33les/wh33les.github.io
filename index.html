Welcome to my website!

About me

I am a trained mathematician working on a career change from academia to industry.

I enjoy knitting and sewing, rollerskating, learning to play bridge, and going to breweries.

I am originally from Colorado Springs, but I grew up in a military family and we moved a lot. Growing up I lived in Colorado, Tennessee, Kentucky, Florida, Germany, and Kansas. As an adult I have moved around a lot, too -- in fact, this is why I created this website! Whenever a contract ends and I switch jobs, it's not clear whether or not my professional website remains. Here is a permanent hub for all of my materials.

I am currently teaching at Centre College in Danville, KY for a one year contract. Thus, I'm on the market for a new job, starting summer 2024! Here is my resumé. Right now I am exploring different opportunities, but for the past year or so I have been preparing for a career in data science.
