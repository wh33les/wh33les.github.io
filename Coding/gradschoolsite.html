Curriculum Vitae

I am a finishing Ph., D. student at Michigan. I earned a Bachelor's in math from Kansas State University in 2008 and an embedded Master's here at Michigan in 2011. I am broadly interested in finding new classes of Gorenstein rings. Their connection to generalized Cayley-Bacharach theorems has also brought to my attention the EGH Conjecture.
